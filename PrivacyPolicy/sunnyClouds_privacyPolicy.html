Privacy Policy for SunnyClouds - iOS Weather Application

Effective Date: August 16, 2023

This Privacy Policy outlines how SunnyClouds, an iOS Weather Application, handles user information. By using SunnyClouds, you agree to the terms specified in this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Use

SunnyClouds does not gather or store any personal information from users. The application relies on Apple's Weather API Service to retrieve weather data based on your location. No user login or registration is required.

Location Information

SunnyClouds requests permission to access your device's location. This permission is essential for providing accurate weather data based on your current location. Your location data is solely used to deliver localized weather forecasts within the app. We do not store or transmit this location data to external servers.

Apple's Weather API Service

SunnyClouds uses Apple's Weather API Service to retrieve weather information. This service allows us to furnish you with current and forecasted weather conditions. Any data obtained from the Weather API is used exclusively to display weather information within the app and is not shared with third parties.

Information Security

We give utmost priority to securing your data. SunnyClouds does not transmit user information over the internet, and no data is stored externally. All weather data is presented within the app and is not retained beyond the current session. We have implemented appropriate technical measures to ensure the security of information acquired from Apple's Weather API.

Children's Privacy

SunnyClouds is not designed for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 13 years old. If you suspect that we have unintentionally collected information from a child under 13, please contact us at sillytrouble@icloud.com, and we will promptly address the matter.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We retain the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy as required. Users will be informed of any changes within the app. We recommend checking this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how SunnyClouds operates, please reach out to us at sillytrouble@icloud.com.

By using SunnyClouds, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and accepted this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, kindly refrain from using the app.
