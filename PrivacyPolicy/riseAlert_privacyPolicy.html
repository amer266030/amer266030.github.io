Privacy Policy for RiseAlert - iOS Alarm Clock Application

Effective Date: August 16, 2023

This Privacy Policy outlines the practices of the iOS Alarm Clock Application "RiseAlert" developed by Amer Alyusuf ("I," "Me," or "My"). This policy explains how RiseAlert collects, uses, and safeguards user information. By using RiseAlert, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Use

RiseAlert does not require or gather any personal information from its users. The application operates entirely offline and does not connect to any external server. There is no need for user login or registration.

Permissions

RiseAlert requests permission to send local notifications to your device. This permission is essential for providing alarm functionality and timely alerts. I do not utilize this permission to collect personal information or access data beyond the application.

Information Security

I prioritize the security of your data. RiseAlert does not transmit any user information over the internet, and no data is stored on external servers. All app-related data and settings are stored locally on your device. I have implemented suitable technical and organizational measures to protect the integrity and security of information stored within RiseAlert.

Children's Privacy

RiseAlert is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 13 years old. If you believe I might have unintentionally collected information from a child under 13, please contact me at sillytrouble@icloud.com, and I will promptly delete the information.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

I reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. In such cases, I will notify users through an in-app update. I encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how I protect your information.

Contact Me

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the practices of RiseAlert, please contact me at sillytrouble@icloud.com.

By using RiseAlert, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms. If you do not agree to this Privacy Policy, please refrain from using the app.
