Coffee Shop App

FlutterBloc/CubitSupabaseOne SignalMoyasar

This application is a comprehensive solution for managing a coffee shop, providing two interfaces for employees and customers. It enables efficient menu management, order processing, and customer service. The app is built using Supabase for backend management, OneSignal for notifications, and Moyasar as a payment gateway.

  • Employee Interface:
    • Menu Management
    • Reports Dashboard
    • Order Management
  • Customer Interface:
    • Menu Browsing
    • Cart & Payment
    • Order Status
