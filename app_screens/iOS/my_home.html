Control your Apple HomeKit-enabled smart devices with ease. This app allows you to manage lights, plugs, humidifiers, garage doors, door cameras, and more. Set up personalized routines and automated actions based on time or sensor triggers. With support for both light and dark modes, it offers a seamless and intuitive way to enhance your smart home experience.
