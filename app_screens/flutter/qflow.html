QFlow suite manages crowd organization at the Tuwaiq Academy job fair with three dedicated applications for different audiences:

  • Visitor
    • Pre-select up to two companies at a time for interviews for better crowd management.
    • Access to company information to make informed interview choices.
    • Timely notifications to ensure punctuality.
  • Company
    • Tools to manage interview queues.
    • Easy access to visitor profiles and CVs for effective interview preparation.
  • Organizer
    • Tools for managing crowd flow.
    • Analytics on visitor/company behavior, and event performance.
