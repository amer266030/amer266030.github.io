This application is a crowdfunding platform designed for individuals legally authorized within the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia to apply for financing or invest in financing opportunities.

Key capabilities include:

  • Role selection as an Investor or Financing Applicant.
  • Multi-factor authentication (PIN, OTP, or biometric login with secure backend verification).
  • KYC verification for identity and background checks.
  • Financing Applicants can apply for financing, receive funding, and make scheduled repayments.
  • Investors can discover, participate in, and track financing opportunities, including monitoring returns on investment (ROIs).
